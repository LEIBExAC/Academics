B.E. Programs (Full-Time)

IET is presently offering Full Time 4-Year B.E. Programs in following six branches as given below:

  • B.E. (Mechanical Engineering)
  • B.E. (Computer Engineering)
  • B.E. (Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering)
  • B.E. (Information Technology)
  • B.E. (Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering)
  • B.E. (Civil Engineering)

B.E. Programs (Part-Time)

IET is presently offering Part-Time 4-Year B.E. Programs in following Five branches as given below:

  • B.E. (Mechanical Engineering)
  • B.E. (Computer Engineering)
  • B.E. (Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering)
  • B.E. (Information Technology)
  • B.E. (Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering)

M.E. Programs (Full-time)

IET is presently offering Full Time 2 Year ME Programmes in the following six branches & specialization as given below:

  • Computer Engineering with specialization in Software Engineering
  • Information Technology with specialization in Information Security
  • Electronics engineering with specialization in Digital Instrumentation
  • Electronics engineering with specialization in Digital Communication
  • Industrial Engineering & Management
  • Mechanical Engineering with specialization in Design & Thermal Engineering

M.E. Programs (Part-time)

IET is also offering Part Time 3 Year ME Programmes in the following six branches & specialization as given below:

  • Computer Engineering with specialization in Software Engineering
  • Information Technology with specialization in Information Security
  • Electronics engineering with specialization in Digital Instrumentation
  • Electronics engineering with specialization in Digital Communication
  • Industrial Engineering & Management
  • Mechanical Engineering with specialization in Design & Thermal Engineering

M.Sc. Programme:

IET is presently offering Full Time 2 Year MSc Programme in following branch

  • Applied Mathematics with specialization in Computing & Informatics

Doctoral Program:

PhD programme is also offered in all disciplines of BE/ME Programmes & all relevant areas of interest. Research component of IET is also strong while a number of research scholars from other reputed organizations such as SGSITS, AICTE, RRCAT, NRCS, IIITA, etc. have registered for PhD programme in various departments of IET.

*All these UG/PG/Doctoral programmes come under Faculty of Engineering of DAVV.
